Protocol
Escalation Path — What To Do When Something Goes Wrong
Follow this path in order. Do not skip steps. Do not guess. Escalate immediately.
RULE: If you are unsure about anything — a payment, a decision, a dispute, a missing document — stop and escalate. Do not act on assumptions. Escalate to King first, then Micky.
1

Attempt to resolve using this SOP

Check if the situation is covered in this document. If the answer is here, follow the documented process exactly.

2

Escalate to King (Matty Mannion) — First Contact

Call or message King directly on 0468 041 477. Provide a clear, brief summary: what happened, what you need, what the deadline is. King is always the first escalation point.

3

Escalate to Micky — Operations Manager

If King is unavailable, contact Micky at micky@renderrender.com.au. Micky handles operational decisions, payment approvals, and scheduling conflicts.

4

Document the issue immediately

Log the issue in writing — what happened, when, who was involved, and what action was taken. File the record to the 06 — COMPLIANCE & ADMIN folder in Google Drive.

5

Do not proceed until authorised

If the issue involves a payment, a dispute, a missing document, or a builder complaint — do not take further action until you have explicit written authorisation from King or Micky.

⚡

Common Escalation Triggers

  • A builder disputes an invoice or refuses to pay
  • A subcontractor invoice amount does not match the agreed rate
  • A job is cancelled or delayed by a builder without notice
  • A payment has been made to the wrong account
  • A subcontractor raises a complaint or dispute
  • Any document is lost, deleted, or cannot be located
  • An email from a builder contains a legal threat or formal complaint
  • Any amount over $5,000 that is not part of the normal weekly payment run
🚫

Never Do These Without Authorisation

  • Process any payment not on the approved weekly invoice list
  • Delete any file, email, or document — ever
  • Agree to a variation or scope change on behalf of the business
  • Issue a credit note or refund without King's approval
  • Share financial information with any external party
  • Respond to a legal letter or formal complaint without King's direction
  • Change a subcontractor's rate or payment terms
  • Commit to a new job or contract without a signed PO

Admin Rules — Always Apply
These rules apply to every task, every day, without exception.
📋

Document & Filing Rules

  • Never delete anything. Archive, do not delete. If in doubt, ask King.
  • Always file original documents — never overwrite or replace an original.
  • Every invoice, PO, and work order must be filed to Google Drive on the day it is received.
  • All document names must include: date, builder/subcontractor name, and document type.
  • PDFs only for all filed documents — no screenshots, no photos of documents.
  • The Google Drive folder structure must be maintained exactly as documented above.
💰

Financial Rules

  • All amounts are ex GST unless explicitly stated otherwise.
  • No payment is processed without Micky's written approval — no exceptions.
  • All invoices must be cross-checked against the job tracker before approval is sent.
  • Aged debtors must be reviewed weekly — any 60+ day debt escalated to King immediately.
  • No credit notes, refunds, or adjustments without King's written authorisation.
  • All financial records must be retained for a minimum of 7 years.
📧

Communication Rules

  • Both inboxes must be checked and actioned within 2 hours during business hours.
  • All external communications must be professional, on-brand, and reviewed before sending.
  • Never make commitments on behalf of the business without authorisation from King or Micky.
  • All builder and subcontractor communications must be via the official email addresses only.
  • Personal email or phone must never be used for business communications.
  • All sent emails relating to jobs, invoices, or payments must be filed to Google Drive.
🔒

Access & Security Rules

  • Google Drive access is for Render King team members only — never share externally.
  • Do not forward internal documents, reports, or financial data to personal email addresses.
  • Report any suspicious emails or phishing attempts to King immediately.
  • All passwords must be stored in the approved password manager — never in email or notes.
  • If you leave the business, all access must be revoked on your final day — notify King.
